Super Custom Snake!

Dive into the reimagined world of a classic: Super Custom Snake! Weave through the map, seizing treats, and lengthening with every capture. But what sets this version apart? It's a meld of personalization and unpredictability.

Within Super Custom Snake, every game is a canvas for your creativity:

Arena Determination: Shape your own battlefield, from intimate challenges to vast expanses. You dictate the scale.

Control the Momentum: Tailor your snake's speed, from a deliberate crawl to a frenetic dash, to suit your mood or challenge yourself.

Tail Mastery: Control how swiftly your snake grows after gobbling treats. Fancy a twist? Adjust the size of snake parts for a distinctive look.

Box Mode Brilliance: Engage in Box Mode for a fresh spin on navigation. A labyrinthine adventure awaits!

Grid Precision: Add a grid overlay for that strategic edge. Perfect for plotting your path with accuracy.

Dark Mode Delight: Switch to dark mode for a serene or nighttime gaming experience. Sleek, stylish, and smooth.

Double or Nothing: The game tosses a coin with every treat appearance, giving you a 50% chance to encounter a regular treat or a double-points delicacy. Are you feeling lucky?

Super Custom Snake isn't just a game—it's a journey of choices and chances. With an array of settings to fiddle with and the thrill of the unexpected, every session is a brand new escapade. Step in, tailor your experience, and let the unparalleled adventures roll!
